Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how our website uses cookies. By continuing to use our website, you agree to the use of cookies as outlined in this policy.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, improve user experience, and provide information to the website's owners.

Cookies Used by Our Website

Our website only uses essential cookies, specifically the Django session cookie, which is necessary for the functioning of the site. No other cookies are used for tracking, analytics, or marketing purposes.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies: These are temporary cookies that remain in your browser's cookie file until you leave the site. They are used to manage your session while browsing our website. Once you close your browser, the session cookies are deleted.

Note: The session cookie does not store any personally identifiable information, nor is it used for tracking purposes. It is solely used for maintaining your session state while navigating through our website.

How to Manage Cookies

Since we only use session cookies, these are automatically removed when you close your browser. If you wish to block or delete cookies from our site or others, you can do so by adjusting your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

For more information on how to manage cookies, you can visit the help section of your browser:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Internet Explorer
  • Safari
